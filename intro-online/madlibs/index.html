A Day at the Park








One fine day while the sun was shining, a young girl named Elizabeth sat on a bench in the park and thought to herself, "Today I shall go swimming." The only problem was, she didn't have her swimsuit with her!

"I can't go swimming without my swimsuit," she told herself. What could she do? Then she had an idea! She would just use her whatchamacallit (you know, that thingamajig) instead! And then she did.
