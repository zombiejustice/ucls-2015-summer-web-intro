I learned how to make Potstickers

This past year I learned how to make potstickers, a tasty East-Asian dumpling. The filling can be many differenty things, but the tricky part is the pastry. It took me two tries to get the dough just right, and about seven tries before I could crimp the edges properly.

I'm really excited to make my own potstickers because I love to eat potstickers with spicy mai ploy sauce. Now that I can make them myself, I can eat potstickers whenever I feel like it! Well, about two hours after whenever I feel like it, but that's good enough.

I'm hoping to make a big batch of pot stickers and freeze them for later to enjoy with my family.
